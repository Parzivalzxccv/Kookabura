Our History

Welcome to Kookaburra Restaurant! Founded in 2010 in the heart of Tokyo, our mission has always been to provide an unforgettable dining experience with a fusion of traditional Japanese flavors and contemporary twists. Let us take you through our journey of passion, growth, and delicious food.

2010 - The Beginning

Our founder, Abil, decided to bring a new wave of modern Japanese cuisine to Tokyo, blending the classic flavors with innovative techniques. Kookaburra was born!

2015 - Expanding Our Reach

As the restaurant's popularity grew, we expanded our menu and opened our second location in the vibrant Shibuya district. This was a major milestone for us.

2020 - Our 10th Anniversary

We celebrated 10 years of culinary excellence, and during this time, we also introduced a new range of vegan and gluten-free options to cater to diverse dietary preferences.

2024 - A Global Presence

Today, we are not only a favorite among locals but also attracting tourists from around the world. Our mission of serving quality and innovation continues.
